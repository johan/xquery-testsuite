XPath and XQuery Full Text 1.0
Test Suite Release Notes

This document contains the release notes for the XPath and XQuery Full Text 1.0 Test Suite.

XQFTTS 1.0.0 (9 July 2009)

  • Initial public release of the XPath and XQuery Full Text 1.0 Test Suite.

XQFTTS 1.0.1 (25 January 2010)

  • Modification of existing tests to reflect grammar changes.
  • Addition of a few new tests for better coverage.

XQFTTS 1.0.2 (9 April 2010)

  • Correction of two tests (ignore-queries-results-q3 and ignore-queries-results-q3b)

XQFTTS 1.0.3 (15 October 2010)

  • Correction of two tests (FTWordsValue-DecimalLiteral and FTWordsValue-IntegerLiteral)
  • Addition of 15 tests (different-sentence-01 through different-sentence-15)

XQFTTS 1.0.3 (15 October 2010)

  • Correction of the results of two tests (FTPosFilter-q2 and FTOrder-q4)
  • Addition of one test and its result (FtPosFilter-q2a)
